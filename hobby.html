My Hobbies

I am passionate about cooking and playing badminton in my spare time. Cooking allows me to explore my creativity and discover new flavors and recipes from different cultures. I enjoy experimenting with ingredients and trying out both traditional and modern dishes. There's something truly satisfying about preparing a meal from scratch and sharing it with friends and family. It brings people together and gives me a sense of accomplishment.

In addition to cooking, I find joy and relaxation in playing badminton. It is not only a great way to stay active, but it also helps me sharpen my reflexes and improve my mental focus. I enjoy the fast-paced nature of the game, the strategic thinking it requires, and the camaraderie that comes with playing. Whether it's a friendly match or a more competitive game, badminton always helps me unwind and keeps me motivated to stay fit.
