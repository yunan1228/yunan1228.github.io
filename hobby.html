My Hobbies

I am passionate about cooking and playing badminton in my spare time. Cooking allows me to explore creativity and discover flavors from different cultures. I enjoy experimenting with ingredients and preparing both traditional and modern dishes. Sharing meals with friends and family brings people together and gives me a sense of accomplishment.

In addition to cooking, I find joy and relaxation in playing badminton. It is not only a way to stay active, but also helps sharpen reflexes and improve focus. The fast-paced nature of the game, the strategic thinking it requires, and the camaraderie make badminton a rewarding pursuit. Whether in friendly or competitive matches, it always helps me unwind and stay motivated.
